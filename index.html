Live Peak Detection

Continuously tracks the best average power for every interval using a rolling window algorithm. Your peak is always current and accurate.

📊

5-Column Table

Interval label, peak watts, W/kg, Normalized Power, and avg heart rate during your best power window — all on one screen.

🎛️

Fully Configurable

Define your own interval list as a comma-separated list of seconds. Only track what matters for your training.

🌗

Dark & Light Mode

Automatically adapts to your device's theme. Yellow values on black for dark mode, blue on white for light mode.

📄

Multi-Page Support

Pages auto-advance every 5 seconds. Tap to advance manually on touch devices.

🔒

100% On-Device

All processing happens locally on your Garmin. No internet connection. No data ever leaves your device.

App settings

userWeight
User Weight (kg)

Used to calculate W/kg. Automatically pulled from your Garmin profile if available; otherwise set manually (30–200 kg).

userFTP
FTP (Watts)

Your Functional Threshold Power. Set manually in app settings (50–600 W).

intervalList
Interval Durations

Comma-separated list of durations in seconds. Default: 5,15,30,60,300,600,1200,1800,3600,7200
